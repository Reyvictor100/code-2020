About Myself

Hi! My name is Alexandra Reynaga. I've always had a passion for drawing, sketching, painting, and all around art.


Since I can remember I have always entertained myself with a pen and paper, and just knew this was something I wanted to pursue into my adolescence. The happiness it brings me to watch myself create something from nothing is amazing and something I look foward to doing as often as I can.


I am so happy you have allowed me to keep my passion going and will be forever thankful to those who support me in what I do.
